Dr. Filip Miljković obtained a master's degree in pharmacy from the Medical Faculty, University of Niš, Serbia in 2014. Following a year spent in professional pharmaceutical practice, he became a licensed pharmacist. In 2016 he joined doctoral studies in Computational Life Sciences at the Department of Life Science Informatics, Bonn-Aachen International Center for Information Technology (b-it), University of Bonn, Germany under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Jürgen Bajorath. He obtained his Ph.D. degree in 2019 for exploring and developing chemoinformatics-driven approaches for kinase drug discovery. Since then, he joined AstraZeneca R&D as a Data Scientist at the Department of Clinical Pharmacology & Safety Sciences in Gothenburg, Sweden.

His research interests include chemoinformatics and computational methodologies for medicinal chemistry and chemical biology, including machine learning and structure-based approaches.
